# Privacy-Policy

BulBul Kids Learning App

Last updated: March 2026

BulBul Kids is a flashcard-based learning application for toddlers developed by Aravind Kumar Rajendran to help young children learn through images and audio in a safe and simple environment.

We value the privacy and safety of children and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles information.

⸻

**1. Information We Collect**

BulBul Kids does not collect any personal information from children or users.

The app:  
	•	Does not require account creation  
	•	Does not collect names, emails, or phone numbers  
	•	Does not track location  
	•	Does not collect photos, contacts, or files  
	•	Does not use analytics to track children  

All learning content in the app works offline on the device.

⸻

**2. Children’s Privacy**

BulBul Kids is designed for children under the age of 13.

We comply with children’s privacy guidelines including:  
	•	COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act)  
	•	Google Play Families Policy   
	•	Apple Kids Category guidelines  

Because we do not collect personal data, children can safely use the app without sharing any personal information.

⸻

**3. External Links**

Some sections accessible from the “For Parents” area may open external links such as:
	•	App Store / Play Store rating page  
	•	WhatsApp feedback link  

These links are intended for parents or guardians only.  

We recommend that children access these sections only with parental supervision.  

⸻

**4. Third-Party Services**

BulBul Kids does not include:  
	•	Third-party advertisements  
	•	Tracking tools  
	•	Behavioral analytics  
	•	Data sharing with third parties  

⸻

**5. Data Security**

Since the app does not collect or store personal data, there is no personal user data stored on our servers.

All app content runs locally on the user’s device.  

⸻

**6. Changes to This Privacy Policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app features change in the future. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.

BulBul Kids Toddlers app is built to be:
